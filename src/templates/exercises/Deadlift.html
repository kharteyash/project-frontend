Deadlift Exercise

Steps To Perform Deadlift Exercise:

  1. Stand with feet hip-width apart, toes under the barbell, and the barbell close to your shins.
  2. Bend at hips and knees, keep back straight, chest up, grip barbell with hands slightly wider than shoulder-width.
  3. Engage your core and brace your abs as if preparing to lift a heavy object.
  4. Inhale deeply, exhale as you lift the barbell off the ground by extending hips and knees.
  5. Keep the barbell close to your body as you lift, maintaining a neutral spine position throughout the movement.
  6. Stand tall at the top of the lift, with your shoulders pulled back and chest lifted.
  7. Lower the barbell back down to the ground in a controlled manner, hinging at your hips and bending your knees.
  8. Keep your back straight and chest up as you lower the barbell, ensuring it stays close to your body.
  9. Repeat for the desired number of repetitions, focusing on proper form and technique.
  10. Start with a lighter weight to master the movement before gradually increasing the load.
