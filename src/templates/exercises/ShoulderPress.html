Shoulder Press Exercise

Steps To Perform Shoulder Press Exercise:

  1. Start by sitting / standing with your feet shoulder-width apart, ensuring a stable base.
  2. Hold a dumbbell in each hand at shoulder height, palms facing forward, with elbows bent and pointed outward.
  3. Engage your core muscles to stabilize your body.
  4. Inhale deeply, then exhale as you extend your arms upward, pressing the dumbbells directly overhead.
  5. Fully extend your arms without locking out your elbows, and pause briefly at the top of the movement.
  6. Lower the dumbbells back down to shoulder height in a controlled manner while inhaling.
  7. Repeat for the desired number of repetitions.
  8. Ensure proper form throughout the exercise, keeping your back straight and avoiding leaning backward or forward.
  9. Focus on using the strength of your shoulders and arms to lift the weights, rather than momentum.
  10. Adjust the weight according to your strength and fitness level, gradually increasing it as you progress.
